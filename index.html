About me

  • Hi, My Name is Aadil Chasmawala and I am a sophomore majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics at New York University Abu Dhabi. I love making games and solving puzzles. Some of my hobbies include watching cricket , playing the guitar and solving chess puzzles.

  • I usually like spending some time of my day just to introspect. It is during these times when I start to appreciate little things all around me. That is how I came up with the idea of this website. This website is dedicated to all the little things that go unnoticed, but upon a closer look , their beauty and significance is revealed and they make us happy.
